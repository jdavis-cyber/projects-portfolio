## 2. Purpose of the Gate Review

*Instructions: Document what this phase gate validates. This section is customized per phase.*

### 2.1 Gate Purpose Statement

*[Select the appropriate phase description below and customize as needed]*

**PHASE 1 - Business Understanding Gate**

This gate review validates that:
- [AI system's business context and strategic alignment are clearly articulated]
- [Stakeholders are identified and their needs understood]
- [Initial risk assessment is complete and documented]
- [Governance scope is defined and approved]
- [Project team is assembled with appropriate competencies]
- [Phase 1 deliverables are complete and of acceptable quality]
- *[Specific entry criteria for Phase 2 are satisfied]*

**PHASE 2 - Data Management & Preparation Gate**

This gate review validates that:
- [Data sources are identified, assessed, and documented]
- [Data governance controls are implemented]
- [Data quality baseline is established]
- [Privacy and regulatory compliance measures are in place]
- [Data preparation and exploration is complete]
- [Training and test data are properly constructed]
- *[Prerequisites for model development are satisfied]*

**PHASE 3 - Model Development & Training Gate**

This gate review validates that:
- [Model architecture and approach are documented and justified]
- [Model development follows established standards and practices]
- [Model training is complete with documented parameters]
- [Initial performance metrics meet minimum thresholds]
- [Version control and model management are in place]
- [Security and privacy controls for model artifacts are implemented]
- *[Model is ready for formal evaluation]*

**PHASE 4 - Evaluation & Validation Gate**

This gate review validates that:
- [Performance testing is complete across all required metrics]
- [Fairness and bias assessment is documented]
- [Robustness and adversarial testing is complete]
- [Model interpretability and explainability are demonstrated]
- [Performance meets acceptance criteria]
- [Risks are documented and mitigations are in place]
- *[Model is approved for production deployment]*

**PHASE 5 - Deployment & Operations Gate**

This gate review validates that:
- [Deployment plan is complete and tested]
- [Operations runbooks and procedures are documented]
- [Monitoring and alerting systems are configured]
- [Incident response procedures are established]
- [User training is complete]
- [Production infrastructure is ready]
- *[System is operational and performing as expected]*

**PHASE 6 - Monitoring & Maintenance Gate**

This gate review validates that:
- [System performance is within acceptable parameters]
- [Monitoring data indicates system health]
- [Drift detection mechanisms are functioning]
- [Incident response processes are effective]
- [Maintenance and updates are current]
- [Lifecycle status assessment is complete]
- *[System continues to meet operational requirements or retirement decision is made]*
